Frequently Asked Questions

What is Astro One?

Astro One is a streaming service that offers TV packs, live sports, and on-demand content for all ages.

How do I subscribe?

You can subscribe through our website, mobile app, or customer service line.

Can I watch on multiple devices?

Yes, up to 5 devices depending on your selected plan using Astro GO.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cards, online banking, and e-wallets like Touch 'n Go and Boost.

How can I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime via account settings or by calling our hotline.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, enjoy a 7-day free trial for new users on selected plans.
