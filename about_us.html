About Us

Astro, Malaysia's leading integrated consumer media entertainment group, was founded with a mission to connect people through the power of entertainment. Since its inception, Astro has grown into a household name, delivering a diverse array of television channels, on-demand streaming, and live events that cater to every taste and age group. Our journey began with a vision to bring world-class content to Malaysian homes, and over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include innovative services like Astro Fibre and Astro GO.

At the heart of Astro is a commitment to quality and innovation. We produce and curate popular local content such as "Gegar Vaganza" and "Andai Tiada Dia," while partnering with global giants like Netflix, Disney+, and more to bring international blockbusters and exclusive series. Our state-of-the-art technology ensures seamless streaming, whether you're watching live sports, kid-friendly animations, or family favorites, all accessible on multiple devices.

With over two decades of experience, Astro has become a trusted brand, blending Malaysian culture with modern entertainment trends. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to enhance customer experience, offering tools like the Ultra Box and mobile apps to keep you connected. As we move forward, Astro continues to push boundaries, delivering entertainment that inspires and unites families across the nation.
